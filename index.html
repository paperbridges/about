Our Team, Our Story

Paper Bridges Corp is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Maryland that encourage students to write letters to orphanages around the world. We started out as a branch in 2019 to support orphans around the world. We strive to provide hope for children in orphanages internationally by writing letters to kids, as well as sending them small gifts to remind them that there are people who care about them. Our letters are written by people of all age groups and walks of life, each full of love and encouragement. We are currently receiving letters from Sanderson High School and Leesville Road High School. We have reached 14 countries, and are continue to make a difference one letter at a time.
