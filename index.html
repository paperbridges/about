Project Statement

We are a group of students at North Carolina that one to make the world a better place! Paper Bridges Corp is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Maryland that encourage students to write letters to orphanages around the world. We started out as a chapter in 2019 to support orphans around the world. We strive to provide hope for children in orphanages internationally by writing letters to kids, as well as sending them small gifts to remind them that there are people who care about them. Our letters are written by people of all age groups and walks of life, each full of love and encouragement. We are currently receiving letters from Sanderson High School and Leesville Road High School. We have reached 14 countries, and are continue to make a difference one letter at a time.

Although nothing can replace the critical role parental love constitutes in a child’s life, we can still hope to ease an orphan’s journey by providing encouragement and support. Our members partner and work with school clubs, orphanages, and communities. This year, we aim to expand our networks by partnering with Vietnamese orphanages like the Vinh-Son orphanage. We welcome all to volunteer for our club, but only official members are eligible to receive certificates. We offer service hours to members for writing 100-500 words per letter. If you would like to partner up or have any questions regarding our organization, please submit a response form to us.
